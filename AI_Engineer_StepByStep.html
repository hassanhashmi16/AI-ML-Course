AI Engineer + ML Engineer — Course Outline

59 modules, zero to complete. Each module: what it is, why it matters, one resource, and its lesson outline.
How to use this: go in order, top to bottom (or jump via the sidebar). Each module's outline (10.1, 10.2, ...) is the actual scope of that module — what to make sure you cover before moving on. Milestones (gold boxes) are the checkpoints that prove you're ready for the next module. AI Engineering = the front end: building products on top of AI. ML Engineering = the back end: understanding what's underneath. Phase 3 = the differentiation layer — evals/cost, inference systems, and multimodal — the depth most AI-engineer portfolios skip. This runs through all three, in order.
